What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs) for relevant keywords and phrases.

Why is SEO relevant?

Often, people use search engines to look up specific products or services using terms such as "web-development in Sydney", "mechanic shop near me", "brown rice 5kg bags", and so on. They do not necessarily have a specific brand in mind. In this case, the businesses that appear on the first page will attract a higher number of visitors compared to the ones that appear on the list later. Hence, for businesses, optimising websites has become an essential part of gaining new customers.



What are its benefits?

heart_plus

By optimizing your website for relevant keywords, you can attract more organic traffic from search engines, leading to increased visibility and potential customers.

Improving your website's search engine rankings helps it appear higher in search results, making it more likely for users to click and visit your site.

Enhancing user experience on your website not only satisfies visitors but also contributes positively to search engine rankings and overall site performance.

SEO efforts can lead to sustainable, long-term results, providing ongoing benefits and visibility for your business in search engine results.

Compared to traditional marketing methods, SEO offers a cost-effective way to reach a targeted audience and drive qualified leads to your website.
